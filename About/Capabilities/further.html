  • functions for the determination of {% include ref.html label="Galois Action" text="Galois groups" %} of rational polynomials,
  • packages [AClib](https://gap-packages.github.io/aclib/), [Cryst](https://www.math.uni-bielefeld.de/~gaehler/gap/packages.php), [CrystCat](https://www.math.uni-bielefeld.de/~gaehler/gap/packages.php) and [CaratInterface](https://www.math.uni-bielefeld.de/~gaehler/gap/packages.php) for crystallographic and almost crystallographic groups,
  • a graphical user interface [XGAP](https://gap-packages.github.io/xgap) that permits, for example, the display of subgroup lattices.
